About Me (Josh T.)

A Millennium Falcon Fanatic

I have loved Star Wars since I was a little boy. I used to make my teachers call me Luke Skywalker in elementary school. Ever since then I have become an expert in the StarWars relm through several means. I have a personal StarWars Lego collection, I have seen every movie 10+ times, I have a group of friends whom I watch every new StarWars film with, and I have a movie quality Luke saber. My favorite ship is definately the Millennium Falcon, although lets not forget my favoritee scene is when the Slave One dumps that beast of a bomb, aka the Seismic Charge. My love for StarWars is the sole reason I began this blog. Let's share our love for the greatest movie franchise around and talk StarWars!
